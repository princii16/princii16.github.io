BellmanFord Algorithm

The project “Bellman-Ford Algorithm” is an algorithm that computes shortest paths from a single source vertex to all of the other vertices in a weighted graph. In such a case, the Bellman–Ford algorithm can detect negative cycles and report their existence. Bellman– Ford proceeds by relaxation, in which approximations to the correct distance are replaced by better ones until they eventually reach the solution. In this algorithms, the approximate distance to each vertex is always an overestimate of the true distance, and is replaced by the minimum of its old value and the length of a newly found path.

Eye in the Sky

Eye in the Sky is a system application where the drone is being controlled with gestures and with a click button. In this project title “Eye in the Sky” we are implementing image processing on images captured by the drone. The main aim of the project is that the drone can identify its subject and move along with it. The video feed from the camera present in the drone will be sent to processing unit that is images captured by the drone will be processed in the form of frames. Once after the processing is done, it will determine how much vertically and horizontally the drone must move and it also calculates how much safe distance it must maintain from its subject. The drone is designed in such a way that it detects human face and human body after which the face or body keypoints will be seen in the laptop in real time.
